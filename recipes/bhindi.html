Bhindi (Okra)

Back

    The Ingredients

  • Okra (Bhindi)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder
  • Oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro)

    Steps

  1. Wash and dry okra, trim the ends, and slice.
  2. Saut√© onions, add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Add sliced okra and cook until tender but not mushy.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
