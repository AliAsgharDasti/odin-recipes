Chicken Biryani

Back

    The Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala
  • Saffron or food color (optional)
  • Mint leaves
  • Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro)

    Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and keep aside.
  2. Soak rice for 30 minutes. Parboil rice and drain.
  3. Saut√© onions, add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  5. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, add saffron/milk color and herbs.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the flavors blend.
  7. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita.
