Chicken Karhae

Back

    The ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes, chopped or pureed
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies, chopped
  • Spices such as cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro) for garnish
  • Optional: Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) for added flavor

    Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Saut√© chopped onions until translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for a minute.
  4. Put in tomatoes, cook till they soften. Add spices, mix well.
  5. Add chicken, coat it with spices. Cook covered on medium heat.
  6. Once chicken is nearly cooked, add green chilies and adjust salt.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and, if using, crushed kasuri methi.
  8. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice. Adjust seasoning to taste.
